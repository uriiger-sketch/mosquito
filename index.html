📖 Data Sources & Methodology

All data is free, public, and peer-reviewed. Each dot represents a city-level vector presence record; radii are pre-validated to keep all scatter on land.

  • WHO World Malaria Report 2025 — country-level burden data (282M cases, 610K deaths in 2024). Top 11 high-burden countries listed explicitly. who.int/malaria/reports
  • PAHO Dengue Multi-Country Outbreak 2024 — 14.4M Americas cases (record). Country breakdown: Brazil 10M+, Argentina 582K, Mexico 559K, Colombia 321K, Paraguay 296K, Peru 272K, Guatemala 189K, Honduras 177K. paho.org
  • WHO Weekly Epidemiological Record WER-10052 (2025) — Global dengue 2024 country data including SE Asia / Western Pacific. who.int/wer
  • CDC ArboNET West Nile Virus 2024 — 1,466 US WNV cases; South Dakota has highest cumulative seroprevalence (13% population). cdc.gov/west-nile-virus
  • ECDC Mosquito Distribution Maps (May 2024) — Ae. albopictus established in 13 EU countries; newly introduced 2024 in France (6 regions), Germany, Netherlands, Portugal, Slovenia. Ae. aegypti introduced Gran Canaria (Spain) 2024. ecdc.europa.eu/mosquito-maps
  • ECDC West Nile Virus Surveillance 2024 — 1,200 European WNV cases; Italy leading (767 cases in 2025 ytd). Northward expansion to Germany and Netherlands documented. ecdc.europa.eu/west-nile
  • Kraemer et al. 2015 (eLife, DOI: 10.7554/eLife.08347) — Largest peer-reviewed global compendium of Aedes aegypti & albopictus occurrence records. Used for Japan, Korea, China, Turkey distributions.
  • WHO Malaria Fact Sheet 2025 — An. stephensi urban spread in Horn of Africa and India. who.int/fact-sheets/malaria

Historical data (1996–2023): Same agencies' archived annual reports, accessed via WHO IRIS, PAHO Pan-Docs, and ECDC Technical Documents repositories. All city centroids verified against OpenStreetMap (OSM) node coordinates. Coastal and island cities use r ≤ 0.22° to stay on land.
